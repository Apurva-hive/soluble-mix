In the game we have 3 levels

level 1
- Initially we have 3 Lives and 0 score
- There are two collectibles for life and health.
    - If we take the life collectible then the value of life will increase.
    - If we takt the health collectible then the percentage of health in life will increase.
- we need to hit the score of 1000 to pass this level.

level 2

- After we cross score of 1000 we reach level 2 and all the game logic would be same as level 1
- If we hit 2000 we move on to the level 3

level 3

- In level 3 if we cross 3000 mark then the boss enemy will appear
- If we are able to kill the boss than we will win the whole game.
